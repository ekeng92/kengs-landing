# Keng's Landing Premium Hydrotherapy Jacuzzi

This is more than a standard hot tub: five seats, multiple jet zones, and adjustable hydrotherapy controls. Take a minute to find the seat and jet pattern that feels best.

## Quick Rules
- Rinse first when possible. Oils, lotion, sunscreen, and makeup cloud the water fast.
- No glass in or around the hot tub.
- Children must be supervised at all times.
- Replace and secure the cover when finished.
- If the water looks cloudy or smells off, message the host.

## Seats & Jet Zones
- Love seat + front single seat: shared jet zone. The sideways throttle knob near the love seat sends flow toward the love seat, the front single seat, or both. Middle = shared flow.
- Foot jets: between the love seat and front single seat.
- Therapy seat: across from the main control panel. Independent jet zone; second-most powerful massage seat.
- Power seat: deepest seat near the control panel. Strongest hydrotherapy position with its own intensity/direction controls.

## Controls
- Jets 1: main jets, High / Low.
- Jets 2: secondary jets, On / Off.
- Jet 3 / therapy seat jets: separate therapy-seat control, not on the main control panel. Look near the therapy seat if you do not see it.
- Labels are swapped: Blower = lights. Lights = bubbles / oxygenator.
- Throttle knobs adjust jet strength/direction; they are not on/off switches.

## Temperature
- 98°F: standby temperature.
- 100–101°F: best for longer relaxing sessions.
- 103–104°F: very hot; use short sessions and get out if lightheaded.
- Heating is slow: about 2°F per hour with the lid closed.

## Longer Stays: Add Shock After Use
If you are staying multiple nights and using the jacuzzi regularly:
1. Leave the cover open when you get out.
2. Add two capfuls of spa shock from the supply cabinet near the hot tub.
3. Run jets with the cover open for about 10 minutes.
4. Turn jets off and replace the cover.
5. Wait at least 30 minutes after adding shock before getting back in.

Please do not add extra shock or mix chemicals.
