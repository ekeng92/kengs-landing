# Keng's Landing Hot Tub Guide

Welcome to the jacuzzi. Please help us keep it clean, safe, and ready for every guest.

## Before You Get In

- Please rinse off first. Body oils, lotions, sunscreen, and makeup make the water foam and get cloudy quickly.
- No glass near the hot tub.
- Children must be supervised at all times.
- Use short sessions if the water is very hot. Exit immediately if you feel lightheaded.

## Using the Jacuzzi

- Open and close the cover gently from the center so the metal assist bar does not bend.
- Jets 1, Jets 2, and Jets 3 control different jet zones.
- The labels are swapped: **Blower = lights** and **Lights = bubbles**.
- Please replace and secure the cover when you are done.

## Longer Stays: Keep the Water Fresh

If you are staying multiple nights and using the hot tub regularly, please add shock after each use.

1. When you get out, leave the cover open.
2. Add **two capfuls of spa shock** from the supply cabinet near the hot tub.
3. Turn the jets on and let them run with the cover open for about **10 minutes**.
4. Turn the jets off and replace the cover.
5. Please wait at least **30 minutes after adding shock** before anyone gets back in.

Please do not add extra shock or mix chemicals. If the water looks cloudy, smells off, or you are unsure what to do, message the host and we will help.

Thank you for helping us keep the jacuzzi fresh and enjoyable.
