Useful details recovered:

- Premium hydrotherapy positioning: five seats, four zones, three jet systems, individual throttle controls.
- Love seat + front single seat share a jet zone and sideways throttle knob.
- Foot jets exist between the love seat and front single seat.
- Therapy seat is across from the main control panel and is the second-most powerful seat.
- Control-panel / power seat is deepest and strongest.
- Blower and Lights labels are swapped: Blower controls lights; Lights controls bubbles/oxygenator.
- Maintenance docs say shock should run with cover open and guests should wait at least 30 minutes after shock.
- Cleaning docs say bromine floater should always have at least two solid tablets, but I left that out of guest-facing guidance to avoid guests adjusting sanitizer tablets.
