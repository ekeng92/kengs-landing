# Keng's Landing Premium Hydrotherapy Jacuzzi

Hot Tub Rules & Guidance
Read before using. Be gentle with controls.

## Safety Rules
- Rinse first if possible. No glass near the tub.
- Supervise children. Exit if lightheaded.
- Replace and secure cover when finished.

## Controls
- Turn knobs slowly. Do not force anything.
- Throttle knobs adjust jet strength/direction.
- Jet 3 / therapy seat control is separate — not on main panel.
- Blower = lights. Lights = air bubbles / blower.
- Sanitizer tablets and maintenance are handled by host/crew.

## Seats & Jet Zones
- Love seat + front single: shared zone; side knob splits flow. Middle = both seats.
- Therapy seat: across from main panel; strong independent massage zone.
- Power seat: deepest seat near panel; strongest hydrotherapy position.

## Longer Stays: Add Shock After Use
1. Leave cover open.
2. Add TWO capfuls of spa shock from supply cabinet.
3. Run jets with cover open for 10 minutes.
4. Replace cover and wait 30 minutes before re-entering.

## Damage & Responsibility
Guests are responsible for damage caused by misuse, forced controls, glass, unapproved chemicals, or ignored instructions. Repair/replacement costs may be handled through Airbnb/VRBO, the lease, deposit, or reservation agreement.

If water looks cloudy, smells off, or controls feel stuck, message the host. Do not add extra chemicals or force controls.
