## Recommended tax-treatment workflow

1. Add a separate review field such as `tax_treatment` rather than overloading `Category` or `Tax Period`.
2. Use four working buckets:
   - `capital_improvement_or_basis_review` — construction/repair/labor/material costs incurred to ready the property before rental service.
   - `startup_or_preopening_cost_review` — pre-opening utilities, setup overhead, travel/meals, and non-capital supplies that are not depreciable assets.
   - `section_179_or_depreciable_asset_review` — durable assets such as golf carts/furniture/appliances that may need receipts and asset treatment.
   - `unresolved_do_not_report` — cash/ambiguous items lacking documentation.
3. Keep every `Status = Review` row in Review until receipt/text/vendor evidence is attached.
4. CPA/SAGE should approve final bucket names and filing treatment before export.
