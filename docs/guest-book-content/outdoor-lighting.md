# Outdoor Lighting & Porch Power

## Back Porch / Patio Lights

The main power switch for the back porch is on the **pine wall behind the TV** (inside the house). Flip it on — the big overhead zappers will come on first.

For the **patio lights**, there are **pull switches outside** on the porch itself. Turn on the main switch inside first, then use the pull switches to control individual patio lights.

### Quick Steps

1. Flip the switch on the pine wall behind the TV (inside)
2. Bug zappers will power on automatically
3. Walk outside and use the pull-chain switches for the patio lights
